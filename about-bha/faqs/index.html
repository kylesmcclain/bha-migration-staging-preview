01-28-25:  Update on OMB Pause of Federal Financial Assistance

We are aware of the recent announcement regarding the Office of Management and Budget (OMB) pause of Federal financial assistance. At this time, we want to assure you that this development does not currently impact Section 8 participants or property owners.

Berkeley Housing Authority is closely monitoring the situation and remains in communication with the U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD). We will provide an update as soon as we receive further guidance or directives from HUD.

Thank you for your patience and understanding.
